I think, the artistic practice has the ability to reflect socio-cultural situations associated with the human behavior, so I consider the art as a tool which allows me to activate thoughts and compare different intimate environments that I find, in this case, between the countries Spain and Germany, where I currently live and work.

And although at first glance my painting could be classified in the genre of “portrait”, if we study it thoroughly, we will discover a pictorial work full of connotations that reflect on the private environment of people.

It is plain that the figure has a great role in my work, but not less than those elements such as chairs, sofas, tables, personal artifacts, clothing, food, etc., which certainly answer the question who are we?-. Far from being able to arrange these components as I please to rebuild a scene and represent it on canvas, I am interested in the spontaneity of these people and the authenticity of the space in itself.

With a biographical-documentary component and a figurative character, I speculate about the lifestyles of people and highlight the strong relationship between subject-object-environment. Thus, I give enough importance to these container elements previously quoted, which we seldom observe but that are a “necessary part” of our domain.
